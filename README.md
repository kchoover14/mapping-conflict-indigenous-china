The Uyghurs are one of 55 ethnic group living within the political boundaries of modern-day China. Their origins are from Central and East Asia, generally around the Tarim Basin. Their political status in China has escalated to the global stage with [accusations against China of attempted genocide](https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-china-22278037). [The US Holocost Museum presents personal stories of Chinese Persecution](https://www.ushmm.org/genocide-prevention/countries/china/chinese-persecution-of-the-uyghurs) against the Uyghurs including identify-based persecution, mass detention, surveillance, efforced sterilization, forced labor, and forced assimilation.
